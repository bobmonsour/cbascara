Your child may need speech / language therapy if they experience difficulties in any of the following:

  • Expressive Language/Oral Expression
  • Accurately producing speech sounds
  • Finding the right words to express themselves
  • Expressing thoughts in an organized manner
  • Effectively communicating with you or their peers
  • Receptive Language/Listening Comprehension
  • Following directions
  • Understanding verbal or written language
  • Responding with off topic responses

What does a therapy session look like?

Therapy will look somewhat different across the ages, but I strive to make therapy engaging and fun (almost as if we weren't doing "therapy" at all!). A session will often include the use of toys, games, maybe some websites/screen time, and movement.

As needed, I will do a brief wrap up at the end of the session, with possible suggestions for homework.
